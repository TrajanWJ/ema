Dock Behavior

The sidebar evolves into a dock. Icons show running indicators (green dots) for apps with open windows. Clicking a dock icon focuses an open window or launches a new one. The Launchpad icon is always first.

App Tiles

Each tile shows live glanceable data from the daemon — unprocessed count, habit progress, last journal entry. V1 apps are full color; scaffolded apps are dashed/dimmed. Click to open as a separate OS window.

Workspace Restore

On startup, EMA reads saved workspace state from the daemon: which windows were open, their positions/sizes. It reopens them automatically. The "running dots" in the dock reflect this restored state.

Window Chrome

Custom title bar with glass ambient background. Drag region on the ambient strip. Custom min/max/close buttons (right side). Each app window gets the same chrome but with its own accent color in the brand area.
