Shared Window Chrome

Every app window gets the same 36px title bar: app icon + name in the app's accent color, breadcrumb showing current view, and min/max/close buttons. The title bar is the drag region.

Independent Lifecycle

Each window connects to the daemon independently via its own WebSocket channel. Closing a window disconnects that channel but doesn't affect others. Reopening reconnects and gets fresh state.

App Accent Colors

Habits: teal · Brain Dump: blue · Journal: amber · Settings: neutral. The accent carries through title bar, active states, and primary actions.
